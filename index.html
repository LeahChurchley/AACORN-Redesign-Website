aacorn

arts, aesthetics, creativity, & organization research network

Welcome to the AACORN website. Our interest is in aesthetics and creativity in organizational research. AACORN members are academics, educators, and artists from Australasia, Europe and the Americas. The visual and performing arts provide the raw material for our work.
